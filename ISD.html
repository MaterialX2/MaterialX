About the Department

Our IS department is committed to providing an education that bridges the gap between technology and business. We focus on equipping students with the skills required to analyze, design, and implement innovative IT solutions that solve real-world business problems.

With a strong emphasis on both theory and practice, our faculty members are experts in their respective fields, providing students with the latest tools and knowledge to thrive in a rapidly changing digital world.

Focus Areas & Courses
  • Information Systems Security: Learn the principles of information systems security and how to protect sensitive data and secure systems from cyber threats.
  • Systems Analysis and Design: Explore methodologies for effectively analyzing and designing IT systems throughout the system development life cycle.
  • Big Data: Learn techniques for handling large datasets and how to analyze and leverage them for strategic decision-making in organizations.
  • Database Management: Understand the fundamentals of database design, management, and administration to organize and retrieve business data efficiently.
  • Data Analysis: Master data analysis skills using modern tools and techniques to turn data into actionable insights.
  • Data Mining: Learn the techniques and tools used to extract patterns and valuable information from large datasets to support business intelligence and decision-making.
  • Cloud Computing: Discover the core concepts of cloud computing, its architecture, and how it enhances business efficiency and flexibility.
Career Opportunities

Graduates from the IS department are in high demand due to their expertise in technology and business. Career opportunities include, but are not limited to:

  • Systems Analyst
  • Work Big Data with Spark
  • Database Administrator
  • Business Intelligence Analyst
  • IT Project Manager
  • Testing Software

According to recent industry reports, the demand for IS professionals is expected to grow by over 15% in the next 5 years, with competitive salaries starting from $60,000 annually for entry-level positions.
